HOBBIES

My main hobby is training and running in triathlons. I have completed ten triathlons. Training is fun! Who doesn't like enjoying the beauty of the morning outdoors. Taking a brisk walk, run, or bike ride is a great way to start the day and reset the mind. A few days in the gym for some weight and balance training keeps the muscles strong. I don't know how many years I will be able to compete in triathlons, but I will always be acive.
